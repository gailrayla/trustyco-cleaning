Our Story

Trusty Co. was born out of a personal journey. In 2024, founder Den stepped back from the daily grind to focus on his mental health. During that time, he found unexpected peace in cleaning — a simple, grounding routine that brought clarity and calm.

What began as a personal form of therapy soon transformed into something bigger. Den realized that creating clean, welcoming spaces could bring the same sense of relief and comfort to others. With that vision, Trusty Co. was founded.

From those humble beginnings, Trusty Co. has grown into a trusted cleaning service with more than 50 clients across the community. Each space we clean is treated with the same care and attention that started it all.

For us, it's not just about dusting or vacuuming — it's about helping people breathe easier, feel lighter, and enjoy their spaces to the fullest.
